Md Nabil Ahsan

I was the Product Designer and Web Developer at my startup called Shoot. I worked on the minimum viable product, a dual-sided marketplace for booking photographers.
I did interviews to determine product needs and then used the research to design and develop the website. It was then user tested on an iterative basis to ensure it was working as intended.

At my UX Design internship at HCSS, I worked on enterprise applications for both the Web and iOS ecosystems for the construction industry. These included applications for managing equipment, monitoring fleets, bidding and estimation on land, etc.
I collaborated with UX Designers, Quality Assurance specialists, and Product Managers to create meaningful experiences for our users.
